This quiz has been designed to get an idea of what you think about different development metrics, the way you perceieve different avenues of development and the role they play in a country's future, as well as, how different development metrics interplay to provide a more general idea of development.

To see where different countries lie, based on your idea of development, take this quiz and press the submit button. Start by typing the names of upto five countries in a comma separated form (ensuring correct spelling and full names for best results) for which you want to see comparison based on your custom etric.The data from this quiz is anonyized and used to analyse how different outlooks of development focus on different metrics. We also present to you, what your peers think of development, anonymized, of course.
